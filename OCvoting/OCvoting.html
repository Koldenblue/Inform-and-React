This Election Voting is Different!

Consider Voting Your Ballot At Home
Every voter will receive a ballot through the mail.
By voting at home, you will avoid crowd (and COVID-19 safety precautions). Return your ballot via secure drop box, by mail(no postage required), or at one of the Orange County Vote Centers.
Please remember to mail your ballot on or before November 3, 2020 at 8pm. Other options include depositing your ballot into one of our secure ballot drop boxes on or before November 3, 2020 at 8 p.m., or return it in-person at any Orange County Vote Center.
Choose to Vote in person over a 5 day period: Oct.30 - Nov.3
Election Day lasts 5 days!
Vote at any Orange County Vote Center(with COVID-19 safety precautions) between Oct.30 and Nov. 3, 2020.
Cast your ballot at any location, stop by on the weekend, and even register to vote.
Orange County Vote Centers
In-person voting begins October 30th and will be open through November 3, 2020.
Vote Centers provide full voting services, all while allowing you to vote at any location.
